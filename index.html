This website is...

a pit stop for when nicotine cravings hit.
Here you'll find interactive support for staying hot and cigarette free. We have a magic button that will slightly bully you with reminders of why you've quit smoking, we've also provided a pal for you to breath with and that guy is always there, breathing freely, which you won't be able to do if you smoke. There's saveable screensavers for mobile and desktop.
and we've included some links to actual resources from, like, the NHS. But I know when I quit, what I really needed was the reminder of why I'm *actually* quitting not just boring stats.

The NHS

The NHS has loads of handy tips and tricks, they've also basically gamefied quitting smoking with their app. Genius!

Allan Carr's - The Easy Way

His language is mildly outdated but this kick started my journey, it's worked for loads of people and I'd say its worth a go! Like with all self help, take what you can, give the rest back.

Reddit

Peer support from the reddit folk! It's good to have a scroll through here when you need a reminder or if you want to check your nicotine flu symptoms are normal

WHO

It's pretty grim reading tbh, which, sometimes, is exactly what we all need. Get some horrific facts and figures in your eyeballs
